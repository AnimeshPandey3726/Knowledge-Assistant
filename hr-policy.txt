ACME Corp — HR Policy (Sample)
Last updated: 2026-05-01
Owner: People Operations

1) Employment classification
- Full-time employees: 40 hours/week standard.
- Part-time employees: <40 hours/week.
- Contractors: not eligible for employee benefits unless specified by contract.

2) Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO)
ACME Corp provides equal employment opportunities to all employees and applicants without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or any other legally protected status.

3) Anti-harassment and non-retaliation
- Harassment is prohibited in all forms (verbal, physical, visual, digital).
- Retaliation against individuals who report concerns in good faith is prohibited.
Reporting:
- Email: people-ops@example.com
- Anonymous hotline: (000) 000-0000 (sample)

4) Leave policy (summary)
- Paid Time Off (PTO): 20 days/year for full-time employees (accrual monthly).
- Sick leave: 10 days/year.
- Holidays: 10 company holidays/year.
- Parental leave: 12 weeks (primary caregiver), 6 weeks (secondary) (sample).

5) Expense reimbursement (high level)
- Business expenses must be pre-approved when possible.
- Receipts required for expenses over $25.
- Reimbursements processed within 10 business days (sample).

6) Performance and conduct
- Expectations: professionalism, confidentiality, compliance with security practices.
- Disciplinary action may include coaching, written warning, suspension, or termination.

7) Data privacy and confidentiality
Employees must protect company and customer data and follow the Security Guidelines document. Access to sensitive data is limited to business need.
